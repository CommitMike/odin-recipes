Mac And Cheese


Description

Mac and Cheese is the ultimate comfort food, featuring tender pasta coated in a rich, creamy cheese sauce. It's simple, satisfying, and loved by both kids and adults alike.

This homemade version delivers a perfect balance of creaminess and cheesiness, making it a go-to recipe for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or cozy evenings at home.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 2000°C.
  2. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1–2 minutes to form a roux.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika if using.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix well.
  7. For a baked version, transfer the mac and cheese to a greased baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and bake for 20 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
